Short Bio
My name is Zhe Zhang. I am a Ph.D. student in the Multiagent Systems and Service-Oriented Computing Lab, which is a part of the Department of Computer Science at North Carolina State University. My advisor is Prof. Munindar P. Singh. I received my Master of Science degree from North Carolina State University and Bacholer's degree in Computer Science from Renmin University, China.
